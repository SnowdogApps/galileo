Wherever you are, you can hop on Wander App to discover your city off beaten paths and learn more about, it's cultural and historical layers.

Less confusion, more fun.

Travelling in new cities is challenging and it’s not always clear where the best spots are. Play a Wander App mission and we’ll take you to the most interesting places whether it’s history, music, art, food or more.

Missions

Missions take you through the best spots of the city and allow you to explore a city from the perspective of a real story.

Tasks

Complete tasks to finish the mission by moving around, playing a wide range of logic games, puzzles and AR-based fun!

Contribute

Become a Creator and craft your own story! Invite your friends to play and test. The best missions will be released to the app!

Compete

Complete missions fast to get the best times on the leaderboard. Earn more XP and coins to level up faster!
